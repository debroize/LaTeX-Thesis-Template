Image types are: Standard images (``Std''), Golden Ratio collages (``GoRa''), Transect collages (``TrCol'') and Plot collages (``PlCol'').
Sample images of TrCol and PlCol in \prettyref{fig:Ap_CollComp_Smpl}.
